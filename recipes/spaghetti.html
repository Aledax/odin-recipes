Spaghetti

Description

Don't touch mine

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put tomato sauce in a pan and heat on low.
  2. Boil a lot of water.
  3. Add a cup of salt.
  4. Add 150g of spaghetti.
  5. Stir occasionally. Cook until cooked.
  6. Drain water.
  7. Add a cup of butter and stir until butter melts.
  8. Put spaghetti on a plate and top with tomato sauce and parmesan.
